Guacamole Recipe

Description

Guacamole is a creamy and flavorful Mexican dip made from ripe avocados, fresh lime juice, onions, tomatoes, and cilantro. It's a perfect combination of tangy, salty, and spicy flavors that make it an irresistible side dish or topping.

This guacamole recipe is quick to prepare and is best served fresh with tortilla chips, tacos, or grilled meats. Its vibrant green color and rich texture make it a crowd-pleaser at any gathering or casual snack time.
